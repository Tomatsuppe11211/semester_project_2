About us

Bidly.com is created by a small group that is into auctions online. We offer an online platform to users all over the world where they can get items for a fair price instead of overpriced.

The Idea of Bidly.com came in 2026 when our small group were bidding on auctions on multiple websites. We keep on working for accessability to most of the auctions on our site.

As an online auction group we keep an eye on over a thousand auctions each day just to make sure the users are getting the real deal. We work every day to secure our platform and keep scammers off our grounds.
